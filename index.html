About Us

Building excellence, one project at a time

Blessing Aluminum Co. Ltd is a leading provider of construction and fabrication services in Lagos, Nigeria. Under the leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Fatoye George, we have built a reputation for excellence in the industry.

We specialize in building contracting, aluminum fabrication, aluminum roofing, and steel fabrication. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for both residential and commercial projects.

Our experienced team and modern equipment allow us to deliver projects on time and to specification. We work closely with clients from concept through to completion to ensure every detail meets your expectations.
